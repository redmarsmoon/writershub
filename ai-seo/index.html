Expert Insights & FAQs

Navigating the complexities of the new AI search era.

What exactly is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)? expand_more
GEO is the next evolution of SEO, specifically designed for a world where AI models (like ChatGPT, Claude, and Google's Gemini) are used to discover and synthesize information. While traditional SEO focuses on ranking in a list of links, GEO focuses on making your content the primary source that these AI models use to generate their summaries and answers.
Why can't I just use my current SEO agency for AI search? expand_more
AI search requires a fundamentally different set of tools and methodologies. Most traditional agencies are still focused on keyword density and backlink volume. Gen AI SEO specializes specifically in the intersection of AI and search, using proprietary methodologies like GEO that traditional agencies simply don't have.
How does AI SEO impact my traditional Google rankings? expand_more
The strategies we use to build topical authority and improve technical structure for AI search are the same factors that Google's traditional algorithms increasingly prioritize. By optimizing for AI search, you are essentially creating higher-quality content in the eyes of all search engines.
Can AI SEO help me rank in ChatGPT and other conversational models? expand_more
Yes, this is a core part of our service. While you can't "buy" your way into a ChatGPT response, you can optimize your digital presence to make it highly likely that the AI will select and cite your brand as the definitive source.
