Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our billing and service levels.

How are academic pages calculated? expand_more
A standard page consists of 275 words, double-spaced, with 12pt font and 1-inch margins. This includes free title and bibliography pages.
What is your refund policy? expand_more
We offer a Money-Back Guarantee if we fail to deliver on time or if the quality does not meet our strictly defined academic/industry standards after two revision cycles.
Do you charge extra for rush orders? expand_more
Yes, orders required within 24-48 hours incur a rush fee to compensate our experts for working on an expedited schedule and prioritizing your delivery.
Are there any hidden fees? expand_more
No. The price you see on the calculator is the final price for the writing service. Plagiarism reports, formatting, and bibliography are always included for free.
