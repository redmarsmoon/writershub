FAQ for New Clients

Everything you need to know about starting your journey with WritersHub Plus.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with your specific needs.

How do you calculate the price?

expand_more

Pricing is determined by three main variables: the difficulty level of the task, the word count required, and your chosen deadline. Our algorithm ensures a fair rate that reflects the expertise of your assigned writer.

Can I request revisions?

expand_more

Absolutely. We offer free unlimited revisions within 14 days of delivery to ensure the content meets your exact specifications and brand voice.

How is the quality guaranteed?

expand_more

Every piece of content goes through a rigorous two-step quality check: first by the writer and then by our internal editorial team for tone, clarity, and plagiarism.

Is my information confidential?

expand_more

We maintain strict NDAs with all our writers and use enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data and intellectual property at all times.
