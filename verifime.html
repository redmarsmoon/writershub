Frequently Asked Questions

What is the core mission of VerifiMe? expand_more
VerifiMe was founded with the mission of democratizing access to the global digital economy. We bridge the gap for talented individuals in developing countries who are excluded from online platforms due to lack of traditional identity infrastructure.
How does VerifiMe ensure the absolute security of user data? expand_more
We employ a multi-layered defense strategy: end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest, multi-factor authentication, and rigorous data minimization practices. Our infrastructure is hosted on cloud-native platforms meeting the highest industry standards.
Is VerifiMe's service legal and compliant? expand_more
Absolutely. VerifiMe is built on a foundation of legal and regulatory compliance. We work with international law experts to ensure our bridge adheres to KYC, AML, and global data privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA.
